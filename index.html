About Us

Ascend, established in 2005, is the largest, non-profit Pan-Asian organization for business professionals in North America. Ascend Cornell, operating under the guidance and sponsorship of New York Metro Regional Council, offer students opportunities for professional development and networking. Ascend Cornell strives to build connections between students and professional member and prepare students for their career objectives and future leadership roles.
